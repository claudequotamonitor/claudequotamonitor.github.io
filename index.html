Everything you need, nothing you don't

Simple by design. Powerful where it matters.

Session usage (5h)

See exactly how much of your 5-hour session window you've used, with a color-coded progress bar.

Weekly usage (7 days)

Track your 7-day quota for all models plus Claude Design separately, each with its own reset timer.

Auto background refresh

Data updates automatically every 10 minutes and after each Claude response. No open tab needed.

Toolbar badge

Your current usage percentage always visible on the extension icon. Turns orange at 70%, red at 90%.

10 languages

Private & open source
